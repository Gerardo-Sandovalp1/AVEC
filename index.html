AVEC

Aerospace Vehicle Engineering Center

Purpose

AVEC is a small research center focused on the conceptual foundations of aerospace vehicle engineering. It serves as the home for the AVE discipline, including the book, concept papers, and long-term technical reflections.

Book

AVE: Foundations of Aerospace Vehicle Engineering
A physics-first, cause-and-effect exploration of aerospace vehicle systems. Full manuscript in development.

Papers

Concept papers will be published here as the discipline develops. Initial topics include:

  • Architecture of aerospace vehicle systems
  • Cause-and-effect engineering reasoning
  • Foundational principles for AVE
