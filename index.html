Aerospace Vehicles Engineering Center

A research center for aerospace vehicle systems

About the Center

The Aerospace Vehicles Engineering Center (AVEC) is a small, founder-driven research center focused on the conceptual foundations of aerospace vehicle engineering. AVEC serves as the home for the AVE discipline, including the book project, concept papers, and long-term technical reflections on vehicle-level architecture and integration.

Research Themes

  • Architecture of aerospace vehicle systems
  • Applied Aerodynamics
  • Advanced Propulsion

Book Project

AVE: Foundations of Aerospace Vehicle Engineering
A physics-first, cause-and-effect exploration of aerospace vehicle systems. Full manuscript in development.

Concept Papers

Concept papers will be published here as the discipline develops. Planned topics include:

  • Vehicle-level architecture and integration
  • Principles of aerospace vehicle engineering
  • Foundational reasoning frameworks for AVE
